Biography

Performer & Educator

Oleksandr Tsiboulski is a classical guitarist of international standing, active as a soloist, chamber musician, pedagogue, and artistic researcher. Born in Rivne, Ukraine, he emigrated to Australia as a young man and studied at the Australian National University, Indiana University, and the University of Texas at Austin, where he held a Fulbright Scholarship.

A prize-winner at twelve international competitions, including the 2006 Tokyo International Guitar Competition, he has performed across Australia, Ukraine, Japan, the United States, the United Kingdom, and Mexico. His two Naxos recordings — Australian Guitar Music (ARIA Award-nominated) and Ponce: Four Guitar Sonatas — are widely regarded as significant contributions to the documented repertoire of the instrument.

Two further recordings are currently in preparation: In Vienna, a disc of previously unrecorded salon works by Mauro Giuliani, and Blue Cobalt, a programme of newly commissioned contemporary music — both recorded in Czernowitz, Ukraine, in 2024 and 2025.

Since 2020, he has taught students in the guitar program at the Melbourne Conservatorium of Music, University of Melbourne. He is the founder and director of the Daylesford Guitar Workshop. He performs on an original 1829 Guadagnini guitar, a 1994 Paulino Bernabé (ex Norbert Kraft), a 2014 Jim Redgate double-top, and a 2025 Sachar Amos "Torres" model guitar.

Pedagogy

Teaching

Oleksandr Tsiboulski teaches selected classical guitar students at the Melbourne Conservatorium of Music, University of Melbourne, by special arrangement, and privately online and in the Melbourne area. He is the founder and director of the annual Daylesford Guitar Workshop.

University
Melbourne Conservatorium
Since 2020, Alex has taught selected students and classes at the Melbourne Conservatorium of Music by special arrangement. Those wishing to work with Alex at a university level are advised to consult about this well in advance as spaces and arrangements may be limited.
Annual Event
Daylesford Guitar Workshop
Founded 2020. An annual intensive workshop for serious guitarists, held in regional Victoria. Past guests have included Eduardo Fernández, Pavel Steidl, and Martha Masters. 2026 edition in preparation.
Private Studio
Individual Lessons
Private lessons available in Melbourne and online for advanced students. Approach combines body-led technique (Carlevaro, Fernández), historically informed performance practice, and somatic awareness. Limited places.

Guest Teaching
